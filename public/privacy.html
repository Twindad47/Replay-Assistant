BETA PRIVACY NOTICE

Privacy and Recording

Replay Assistant is being tested as a privacy-first coaching tool. This notice explains how the current beta works.

Saved replays stay on the controller device

When a coach saves a replay, the video and its thumbnail are stored in that controller browser’s local storage. Replay Assistant does not upload saved replay files to the Render web server.

Live camera connections

Private session codes keep cameras separated from other sessions. The Render service coordinates the connection between devices. Video normally travels directly between camera and controller devices through WebRTC. When a TURN relay is configured and required by the network, video may pass through that relay provider.

Adults are responsible for recording permission

The adult operating Replay Assistant is responsible for obtaining permission before recording an athlete, especially when the athlete is a minor. Do not share private session codes or downloaded clips with people who should not have access.

Browser storage can be cleared

Saved replays can disappear if browser data is cleared, the device is reset, or storage is removed. Download important clips as backups.

Beta feedback

Feedback submitted through the beta form is sent to the Replay Assistant server logs and may include optional contact information. Do not include sensitive information in feedback.

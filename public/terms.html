BETA USE NOTICE

Use Replay Assistant Responsibly

Replay Assistant v0.6.0 is an early testing build. Features, storage behavior, and availability may change while the product is being developed.

Training aid, not a safety system

Replay Assistant is a video review tool. It does not replace qualified coaching, proper spotting, safety equipment, medical advice, or supervision.

Recording responsibility

Only record people when you have permission and when recording is allowed at the facility. The person creating the session is responsible for how recordings are used, downloaded, or shared.

Beta availability

The service may restart, disconnect, or become temporarily unavailable. Test important workflows before relying on Replay Assistant during a practice or event.

Protect important clips

Saved clips are stored in the controller browser. Download important replays because browser storage is not a permanent backup.
